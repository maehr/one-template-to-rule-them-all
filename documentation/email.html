Hi all,

We’re excited to be publishing proceedings papers from the Digital Humanities Tech Symposium in the new ACH open access conference series Anthology of Computers and the Humanities. You’re receiving this email because you indicated your interest in submitting a short article based on your presentation.

As a reminder:

Proceedings articles should be 4 to 6 pages in length.

Articles need to be submitted by August 3, 2025. We highly recommend having a draft ready in time for the conference.

Articles will be reviewed with potential requests for revisions.

Reviewed articles will be published on the proceedings website with a DOI for each article.

For your submission please use the ACH Anthology LaTeX template (attached, and also available as an Overleaf template). If you are unable to use LaTeX, let us know so we can advise on an alternate format.

As a next step, please reply to this email with the following information by June 28, 2025.

Confirm you are still planning to submit an article and are able to do so by August 3, 2025.

Confirm that you are able to review other submissions. If you are unable to review other submissions it will not preclude you from submitting, but we need to know.

We look forward to seeing you at the conference,

Digital Humanities Tech Symposium Program Committee
